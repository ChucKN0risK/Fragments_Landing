What we want to change

  • Designers and developers are separated

    At Fragments, we believe great products are build when design and technology are brought together. The digital world is moving to a new agile process killing the old waterfall one.

  • Inconsistent brand UIs

    Good products degrade as they grow and get harder to maintain. More people join, some earlier folks leave, and clarity of design vision deteriorates.

  • There is no shared place to collaborate

    We all seek for a perfect synchronisation between our visual design tools and our final product. Still, every people involved in the process don’t share the same language or resources.

What we help you do

  • Bringing designers and developers closer

    Sharing and maintaining UI Libraries across Sketch files is hard. The Fragment Sketch Plugin will help you create a design library and share it with your team members.

  • Finally a place to write specs

    Fragment helps you write specs easily with a an intuitive text editor.

  • Generate assets for iOS, Android and the web

    Your brand assets such as colors, fonts, spacings, breakpoints are synced and shared across all of your platforms and products.

  • Sync your assets with your code repository

    Just link your code repository to the Fragment Sketch Plugin so designers can update your brand assets.

  • Get notified via the Fragment Slack app

    Everytime your UI Library is updated your team members notified on Slack.

  • Speed up your team productivity

    With a design system no need to reinvent the wheel when starting a new project. It allows your team to focus on what matters most: designing.
